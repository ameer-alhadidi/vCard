Design Notes

per sketch SWC-001
  • Stack order (top → bottom): outer lid → compartment lid (1.00" tall) → food container (Ø3.00" × 4.00").
  • Compartment lid is an optional add-on: two D-shaped wells hold sauce and breadcrumbs separate from the hot dish; a raised centre channel between them holds a spoon or fork.
  • Seal runs around the compartment lid's rim, closing the wells against the underside of the outer lid and the cup mouth so sauces can't leak in transit.
  • Microwave use: lift out the compartment lid (with cutlery and cold sauces) and heat only the paperboard food container, then re-seat the lid to dress the dish.
  • Compartment lid nests ~Ø2.90" inside the Ø3.00" mouth; cup tapers to ~Ø2.50" at the base for stacking.
